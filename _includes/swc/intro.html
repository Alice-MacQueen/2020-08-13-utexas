Software Carpentry aims to help researchers get their work done in less time and with less pain by teaching them basic research computing skills. This hands-on three hour Carpentry lesson will cover basic concepts and tools, for version control and code management, using Git and GitHub. Participants will be encouraged to help one another in the collaborative notetaking document and to apply what they have learned to their own research problems.

For more information on what we teach and why, please see our paper "Best Practices for Scientific Computing".
